Why I think Astrology is fake

Astrology suggests that celestial bodies affect our lives in a way that doesn't stand up to scientific scrutiny.

Astrology claims that the positions of stars and planets at the time of our birth determine our personality traits and future. However, there's no scientific evidence to support this idea. The forces of gravity and other influences that astrology suggests have no measurable effect on human behavior or life outcomes. Furthermore, astrologers have failed to demonstrate any repeatable, verifiable connection between astrological signs and personality traits or events in people's lives.

In essence, astrology operates on vague generalities and broad statements that could apply to anyone, which is why people often feel like astrology predictions fit them. The lack of consistency in predictions and the inability to scientifically test astrology make it more of a pseudoscience than a legitimate field of study. So, while astrology can be fun or entertaining, I think it's best to treat it with skepticism and rely on more evidence-based practices to understand our world and ourselves.
