A Conceptual Hypothesis for Microbial Survival on Rogue Planets

A structured hypothesis exploring whether dormant microorganisms could survive long-duration interstellar travel inside rogue planets and potentially reactivate in a new star system under favorable conditions.

It connects microbial dormancy, planetary ejection physics, and interstellar environmental constraints.

Not a claim. Not proof. A framework for questioning biological persistence beyond planetary systems.
