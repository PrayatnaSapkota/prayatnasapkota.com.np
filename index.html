Who am I?

I’m Prayatna, just a curious guy trying to figure out how everything in this universe works. I’ve always been fascinated by space and physics. I don’t really follow beliefs or superstitions coz I like to question things and understand them for myself. Most days, you’ll find me reading books or just staring at the night sky, wondering how far curiosity can actually take me. Maybe one day, I’ll find out.
